What is multibooting?

Multibooting is the process of having multiple OSes booting off a same device and physically present on that device. You might say "well duh!" but some people do not know that you can only boot one OS at a time. Contrary to virtualisation, multibooting does NOT load all OSes at the same time but you turn off one to get to the other. Multibooting can be on any machine, whether it's a virtual one or a real one, as long as the firmware is able to boot either of the OSes, it's good.
